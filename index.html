How Did It All Begin?

My passion for computers first started when I was around 9 years old and noticed my first computer had been infected with a severe virus that took control of my cursor and immediately navigated it to close any browser window I tried to open!! Frustrated with the helplessness of not being able to control MY own computer I began taking it apart to physically see if I could find and fix the problem...Obviously with no luck I began researching what the problem could possibly be during Free-Time at school. Eventually learning about hardware vs software and how it they worked in tandem I performed a (what I now know is the lazy way out) complete system reset, but hey, to me it was still a victory! From there I was hooked and wanted to learn all I could about computers and technology in general!
